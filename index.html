Daniel

My name is Dan, and I am the owner and operator of Lawnicures, L.L.C.

In 2012, I aspired to create a small, family owned and operated business where I could spend time working alongside my son (then twelve), getting him outdoors, allowing him to earn an income, and teaching him about so much more than just “yard work”. My desire was to help Josh learn the business from the inside-out, instilling a work ethic, and encouraging him to make good choices as he learned professionalism, and the pride to be had in a job well done.

I, personally, have had a love for lawn and landscaping since I was a young teen. I knew this would be my calling from the time I was sixteen, and worked as a professional landscaper until I was twenty-six. In my late twenties, I went into carpentry, and briefly enjoyed a few other trades. I always knew I would come back to this profession, and have since enjoyed creating a company from the ground-up. Looking back, and seeing where I am today, having built this business with my son beside me the whole way, is priceless.
